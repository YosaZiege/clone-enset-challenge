About ENSET Challenge 2026

Morocco's leading student technology competition focusing on AI and Cybersecurity

ENSET Challenge 2026 stands as Morocco's premier student technology competition, focusing on the critical domains of Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity. This year's event features an AI-themed Hackathon centered on "AI for Economic & Social Development" alongside a high-stakes Cybersecurity Capture The Flag (CTF) competition. Presented as a cornerstone of the Global Entrepreneurship and Innovation Week (GEIW) at ENSET Mohammedia, this initiative is collaboratively organized by three dynamic student clubs: N7Geeks, GDG On Campus ENSET-M, and N7SEC, uniting their expertise to deliver an exceptional technological showcase.

Our Mission

We empower Morocco's next generation of tech innovators by providing a platform where theoretical knowledge transforms into practical solutions. Our mission is to drive technological advancement through AI applications that address real economic and social challenges, bridge the gap between academia and industry, and cultivate future tech leaders who will shape Morocco's digital landscape.

The Experience

ENSET Challenge offers a uniquely immersive experience where competition meets collaboration. Participants engage in hands-on problem-solving with direct access to industry mentors and professionals. The vibrant atmosphere, fueled by the energy of passionate student organizers, creates an environment where innovation thrives and professional connections form.

Student-Powered

The driving force behind ENSET Challenge is the synergistic collaboration of three pioneering student clubs: N7Geeks, GDG On Campus ENSET-M, and N7SEC. Each brings unique expertise—software development, Google technologies, and cybersecurity respectively—creating a powerhouse of student talent and leadership that showcases the best of ENSET Mohammedia.
