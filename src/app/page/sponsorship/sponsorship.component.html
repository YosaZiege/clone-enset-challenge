In-Kind Sponsorships

We enthusiastically welcome in-kind contributions that enhance the ENSET Challenge experience. These non-monetary sponsorships are valued equally to financial support and receive corresponding recognition benefits.

  • Technology resources (software licenses, cloud credits, hardware)
  • Prizes for competition winners
  • Catering or refreshments for specific event segments
  • Expert judges or mentors for competitions

The value of in-kind sponsorships will be assessed and matched to the appropriate sponsorship tier, providing your organization with all the corresponding benefits and recognition.
